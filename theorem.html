Theorem of the Week

Be \(b \in \mathbb{R}\).
If \(\phi(x) + \phi'(x) \to b\) as \(x \to +\infty\), then \(\phi(x) \to b\) and \(\phi'(x) \to 0\).

G. H. Hardy
